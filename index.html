Where Curiosity, Code, and Culinary Arts Meet in Sacramento

Our Mission: To Build the Future, One "Aha!" Moment at a Time.

At Pixel & Whisk, we know that the best creations —- whether in the kitchen or in code -— start with the right ingredients. Our mission is to provide the perfect recipe for young innovators to thrive.

We start with a foundation of hands-on learning, where every project is a chance to get creative and build something real. We mix in a generous amount of personalized support, ensuring every student feels seen, heard, and empowered to learn at their own pace. Finally, we sprinkle in a ton of fun, creating a welcoming space where curiosity is the main course.

By perfecting this recipe, we're not just teaching tech skills; we're breaking down barriers and building a more inclusive future. We're dedicated to opening doors so that every student has the chance to discover their passion and become a confident, creative problem-solver.

Bio

What do a perfect plate of pasta and a brilliant video game have in common? They both start with a dash of creativity, a pinch of logic, and an expert guide who makes the process exciting.

For Alena, the journey from managing boutique hotels in Italy to building worlds in code wasn’t a career change -— it was a new way to express a lifelong passion for creation. Raised in a family of engineers and creators, she was tinkering with tech from a young age. But it was her time in the hospitality world that taught her the most important lesson: the best recipes, like the best code, are brought to life with joy and hands-on guidance.

Today, Alena combines the precision of a chef with the playful spirit of a gamer to create a one-of-a-kind learning experience. Her classes are more than just tutorials; they are creative labs where students feel safe to experiment, empowered to build, and thrilled to see their ideas come to life. It's this unique blend of structure and spark that has made Alena a favorite among students, parents, and fellow educators alike, as she nurtures the next generation of curious minds and confident creators.

If you're interested in learning more about Alena's background and experience, her full resume can be found via her LinkedIn profile.

Sacramento Kids' Classes: Coding, Game Design, & Culinary Science

What sparks your curiosity today? Dive into these cutting-edge technologies and discover new skills and possibilities!

Block Coding in Scratch

Ages 8-10 Beginner

Scratch is an entry-level language that teaches coding fundamentals using predefined blocks, perfect for young creators.

Roblox Game Design

Ages 10-14 Beginner

Design and create interactive games in Roblox using the Lua scripting language, covering game mechanics and UI design.

Python 101

Ages 11+ Intermediate

An introductory course on the basics of Python programming, including variables, functions, and input/output operations.

Intro to Web Development

Ages 12+ Intermediate

Learn to build and style websites from scratch using HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript with real-world applications.

VR Adventures: Game & World Design

Ages 12-15 Intermediate

Create immersive VR experiences and games using CoSpaces, introducing 3D modeling, animation, and interactive design.

DIY Gadgets with Raspberry Pi

Ages 11+ Advanced

Go beyond the screen and build real-world smart gadgets! This course teaches students how to combine simple electronics and Python code on the Raspberry Pi to create custom projects.

Little Chefs: Culinary Science

Ages 5+ Any Skill Level!

From pizza science to dessert decorating, young chefs will learn kitchen safety and basic culinary skills in a fun, hands-on environment.

Curiosity Club: Awesome Science

Ages 6+ All Skill Levels!

Get ready to mix, build, and launch! This course brings science to life with exciting, hands-on experiments, from erupting volcanoes to homemade slime.

Future Founders: Digital Business

Ages 9+ Intermediate

Got a great idea? This course teaches the basics of turning a passion into a digital business, from branding and web design to online marketing.
